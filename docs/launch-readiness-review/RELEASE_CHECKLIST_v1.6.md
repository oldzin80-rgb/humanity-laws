## v2.0 Operational Readiness Reference

The v2.0 Operational Readiness Program expands this checklist into evidence-driven workstreams:

- Production Platform
- Product Experience
- Operations & Governance
- Evidence & Launch Control

Release checklist items must not be marked complete because they are planned or documented. Completion requires evidence appropriate to the item: code, tests, review records, deployment logs, operational validation, recovery proof, documentation, and approval records where applicable.
