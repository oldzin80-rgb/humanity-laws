# Humanity Laws V1 Publishing Launch Package

Status: PUBLISHING MATERIALS ONLY

This package does not add product features. It does not change the Humanity Laws book/source text. It does not change commerce, authentication, Stripe, membership, Supabase, DNS, or deployment logic.

## Publishing principles

- Tell the truth plainly.
- Do not create fake urgency.
- Do not use fake testimonials.
- Do not exaggerate outcomes.
- Do not claim the platform is finished.
- Do not imply professional medical, legal, financial, or therapeutic replacement.
- Invite people into the book, the mission, and the early community with humility.
- Make clear that V1 is the beginning of a living work.

## Short founder story

Humanity Laws came from a long personal journey of searching, writing, reflecting, building, and trying to understand what helps people live with more truth, gratitude, responsibility, and love.

I did not want to create another noisy product. I wanted to build something that felt more like a home: a place where people could read, reflect, return, and grow.

The book remains the foundation. The digital experience exists to support it, not replace it. Over time, Humanity Laws will continue to grow through real feedback, real use, and careful stewardship.

My hope is simple: that someone who enters Humanity Laws leaves a little clearer, calmer, more grateful, and more capable of taking the next right step.

## Launch FAQ

### What is Humanity Laws?

Humanity Laws is a book-centered digital home built around reflection, stewardship, human dignity, gratitude, responsibility, and growth.

### Is V1 the finished version?

No. V1 is the first public foundation. The experience will improve through real reader and member feedback.

### What is included in V1?

V1 is focused on the website, book experience, digital purchase path, membership path, account/login foundation, dashboard, Spark, founder message, and community foundation.

### Is the book being changed by the digital experience?

No. The book remains the source. The digital experience supports the book; it does not replace or rewrite it.

### Is Humanity Laws medical, legal, financial, or professional advice?

No. Humanity Laws is educational, reflective, and supportive. It does not replace qualified professional care, judgment, or advice.

### What is Spark?

Spark is a simple reflection practice: one prompt, one moment, one next step.

### What is membership?

Membership is the path for people who want access to the deeper digital experience and ongoing Humanity Laws development.

### Is the hardcover available?

Only if a real fulfillment path has been verified. If fulfillment is not active, hardcover should remain clearly marked as coming soon.

### How can I help?

Read honestly. Share thoughtfully. Leave real feedback. If the work helps you, tell someone who may benefit from it.
