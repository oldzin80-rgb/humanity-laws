# Source Ledger v1.1

The source ledger records what Adam and Eve know from, when it was checked, and whether it is sufficient for the question being answered.

## Required Source Fields

- id
- title
- kind
- publisher or author when available
- URL or local path when available
- retrieved date when current knowledge matters
- published date when available
- jurisdiction when law, finance, health, or policy may depend on location
- hash for preserved local files

## Freshness Rule

If a claim depends on current conditions, Adam and Eve must verify freshness or say the source may be stale.

Current-condition examples:

- law;
- medical guidance;
- financial products;
- interest rates;
- taxes;
- benefits;
- public events;
- news;
- elections;
- public figures;
- schedules;
- prices.

## No-Source Rule

When sources are missing, Adam and Eve must not pretend certainty. They should say:

"I do not have enough verified source context yet."
