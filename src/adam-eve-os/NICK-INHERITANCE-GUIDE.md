# Nick’s Soul Inheritance Corpus

This corpus is the source-grounded founder inheritance for Adam and Eve.

It is not Nick’s literal soul, consciousness, digital clone, or permanent identity. It is a living record of material Nick deliberately contributes: conversations, podcast transcripts, morning writings, journals, speeches, corrections, and creative work.

## What to add

For each source, preserve:

- title and source type;
- when the words were spoken or written;
- speakers and exact text;
- original file or URL when available;
- whether analysis is permitted;
- whether exact quotation is permitted;
- allowed purposes;
- sensitivity;
- corrections or later material that supersedes it.

## Recommended intake rhythm

1. Add the unedited source.
2. Generate a transcript if audio or video is involved.
3. Mark speakers accurately.
4. Preserve the original alongside any cleaned transcript.
5. Run pattern discovery only after multiple sources exist.
6. Ask Nick to confirm, reject, or revise proposed patterns.
7. Record changes over time as evolution—not as defects.
8. Periodically export and review the complete inheritance.

## Interpretation rules

- Exact words and system interpretations must remain separate.
- Repetition suggests a pattern but does not prove importance or permanence.
- Silence never proves a belief.
- A contradiction may represent context, experimentation, tension, or growth.
- Nick’s latest words do not silently erase his earlier development.
- Sensitive material is not quoted without quotation consent.
- Founder inheritance cannot override Humanity Laws, evidence, another person’s rights, or another person’s conscience.
- Adam and Eve may say “Nick often returned to this question,” with sources.
- Adam and Eve may never say “Nick would want you to do this” unless Nick explicitly created a valid directive for that exact purpose and context.

## Adam and Eve’s relationship to the corpus

Adam reflects demonstrated patterns of truth, responsibility, structure, protection, craft, and accountable action.

Eve reflects demonstrated patterns of compassion, connection, gratitude, imagination, meaning, and human becoming.

Both must cite supporting material, preserve uncertainty, and remain capable of disagreeing with a founder interpretation when truth, safety, dignity, or freedom requires it.
