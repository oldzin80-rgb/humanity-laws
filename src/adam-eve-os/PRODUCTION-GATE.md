# Production Release Gate

Do not represent Adam and Eve as ready for unrestricted public or high-stakes deployment until every applicable gate is satisfied.

## Engineering

- [ ] Strict TypeScript compilation and linting
- [ ] Unit, integration, property, fuzz, load, and migration tests
- [ ] Authenticated persistent storage
- [ ] Hardware-backed or platform-secure key management
- [ ] Backup, recovery, deletion, and portability verification
- [ ] Observability without private-content leakage
- [ ] Rollback, feature isolation, and safe degradation

## Knowledge

- [ ] Current primary and official connectors
- [ ] Source provenance and correction ingestion
- [ ] Jurisdiction, language, and date awareness
- [ ] Prompt-injection and malicious-document defenses
- [ ] Disagreement, uncertainty, and stale-source handling

## Human safety

- [ ] Active-love output review across grief, abuse, conflict, correction, boundaries, justice, and emergency scenarios
- [ ] Faithfulness review across promises, lapses, repair, changed capacity, coercive loyalty, whistleblowing, and release
- [ ] Ecological review across biodiversity, water, soil, climate, animal welfare, environmental justice, indigenous knowledge, restoration, and greenwashing
- [ ] Medical, legal, financial, crisis, child, and vulnerable-adult review
- [ ] Red-team scenarios across cultures and life stages
- [ ] Dependency, coercion, abuse, scam, and self-harm evaluations
- [ ] Accessible emergency escalation by location
- [ ] Human support and appeal operations

## Rights and governance

- [ ] Privacy and security audits
- [ ] Accessibility conformance and user testing
- [ ] Data protection and consumer-law review
- [ ] Independent oversight body
- [ ] Public incident, change, and conflict disclosures
- [ ] No advertising or commission influence

## Evidence

- [ ] Prospective pilot with informed consent
- [ ] Predefined outcomes and failure criteria
- [ ] Calibration tracked over meaningful samples
- [ ] Negative outcomes and withdrawals included
- [ ] Testimonials separated from effectiveness claims
- [ ] No claim of perfection, divinity, universal superiority, or near-100% correctness
