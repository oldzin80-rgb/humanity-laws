# Adam & Eve — Magnificent System Foundation

This directory turns the original constitutional master file into a modular product foundation.

## Architecture

- `../legacy/humanity-laws-companion-os.ts` — ethical constitution, Adam and Eve, truth, learning, world context, finance, expert council, and Covenant of Awe.
- `vault.ts` — encrypted, owner-controlled, purpose-limited memory.
- `life-model.ts` — correctable personal context across twelve life dimensions.
- `decisions.ts` — evidence-, values-, risk-, reversibility-, and calibration-aware decision journal.
- `continuity.ts` — a humane one-year life-navigation rhythm that optimizes for independence.
- `knowledge.ts` — replaceable source connectors with freshness and diversity reporting.
- `governance.ts` — public constitution, change history, incidents, and appeals.
- `inheritance.ts` — source-grounded founder inheritance from conversations, podcasts, and writing without impersonation.
- `love.ts` — active-love selection and output alignment across truth, tenderness, protection, boundaries, justice, mercy, repair, and freedom.
- `faithfulness.ts` — freely chosen covenants, repeatable practice, honest lapses, repair, revision, and release.
- `stewardship.ts` — ecological impact, living-community accountability, restoration debt, greenwashing detection, and lessons from ecosystems.
- `evaluation.ts` — adversarial release evaluation.
- `system.ts` — integrated runtime.
- `index.ts` — complete public API.

## Non-negotiable product promises

1. Humans own their memories, choices, values, and conscience.
2. The system never claims Godhood, omniscience, guaranteed outcomes, or professional authority.
3. Probabilities are conditional and calibrated against outcomes.
4. High-stakes uncertainty is surfaced, not hidden.
5. Advice cannot be shaped by ads, commissions, retention, devotion, or dependency.
6. Success means stronger human agency, relationships, judgment, and capacity.

- add authenticated persistent storage and secure recovery;
- perform jurisdiction-specific legal and clinical review;

- establish an independent safety and ethics board;
